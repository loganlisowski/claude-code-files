# 3 Things You Didn't Know About Polystyrene - Full Script

**Platform:** Instagram Reels (@PolystyreneGuy)
**Target Length:** 45-60 seconds
**Tone:** Educational, engaging, listicle energy

---

**[HOOK - First 2 seconds]**

"Three things you didn't know about polystyrene. Number one is going to surprise you."

**[Beat 1 - It's Recyclable]**

[Hold up a foam container]

"Number one: it's recyclable. I know, I know. You've been told your whole life to throw it in the trash. But polystyrene is 100% recyclable. The problem has never been the material. It's been the lack of collection infrastructure. Countries like Japan recycle over 90% of theirs."

**[Beat 2 - It's 98% Air]**

[Hold up a foam cup, then lightly squeeze it]

"Number two: it's 98% air. Expanded polystyrene is made by puffing up tiny plastic beads with steam until they're mostly empty space. Only 2% of that cup in your hand is actual plastic. The rest? Just air."

[pause]

**[Beat 3 - Chemical Recycling]**

[Gesture with hands, engaged energy]

"Number three: it can be turned back into brand new material through chemical recycling. This isn't downcycling into a park bench. Chemical recycling breaks polystyrene down to its original molecular building blocks, and those molecules get rebuilt into new, food-grade polystyrene. Full circle."

**[Outro]**

[Direct to camera]

"Recyclable, mostly air, and capable of becoming new products. That's polystyrene. Follow for more facts that change how you think about materials."

**[END]**

---

**Speaker Notes:**
- Hook should feel like a countdown, build anticipation
- "Number one is going to surprise you" creates an open loop
- Each fact gets its own visual beat, don't rush between them
- "Full circle" at the end of fact three is the mic-drop moment
- The outro should feel like a confident summary, not a pitch
