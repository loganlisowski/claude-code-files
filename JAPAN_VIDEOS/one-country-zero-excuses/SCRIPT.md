# One Country Zero Excuses - Full Script

**Platform:** Instagram Reels (@PolystyreneGuy)
**Target Length:** 45-60 seconds
**Tone:** Assertive, motivational, call-to-action energy

---

**[HOOK - First 2 seconds]**

"One country recycles over 90% of its polystyrene. Zero excuses left."

**[Beat 1 - The Fact]**

[Cut to b-roll of Japan cityscape]

"Japan. A country of 125 million people recycles more than 90% of all the polystyrene it uses. Not 20%. Not 50%. Over 90%."

**[Beat 2 - How They Did It]**

[Cut to recycling infrastructure visuals]

"They didn't do it with some futuristic technology. They did it with three things: clear sorting rules, dedicated collection systems, and processing facilities that actually accept the material."

[pause]

"That's it. Infrastructure and intention."

**[Beat 3 - The US Reality]**

[Back to camera, slightly more intense]

"In the US, we have the same technology. We have the same material. We have companies ready to process it. But most cities won't even put polystyrene in their curbside programs. [pause] Not because it can't be done, but because nobody's demanded it yet."

**[Beat 4 - The Call to Action]**

[Direct to camera, close framing]

"Japan proved the model works. The excuses are gone. It's time for US cities to step up, adopt polystyrene recycling, and stop sending recyclable material to landfills."

[pause]

"Share this if you agree. Follow for more."

**[END]**

---

**Speaker Notes:**
- The hook should hit hard, confident and declarative
- "Not 20%. Not 50%. Over 90%." needs rhythmic delivery, each number punchy
- "Infrastructure and intention" is the quotable line, pause slightly before and after
- The call to action should feel empowering, not angry
- Close with conviction, you believe this is achievable
