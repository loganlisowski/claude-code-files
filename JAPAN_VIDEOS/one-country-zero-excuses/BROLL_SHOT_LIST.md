# One Country Zero Excuses - B-Roll Shot List

---

| # | Shot Description | Why It Works | Duration |
|---|-----------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Aerial or wide shot of a Japanese city skyline (Tokyo, Osaka, or similar) | Establishes scale and location immediately | 3s |
| 2 | Street-level shot of a clean Japanese commercial district | Sets the visual tone for a well-managed society | 2-3s |
| 3 | Japanese recycling collection point with clearly labeled polystyrene bin | Core visual proof of the dedicated system | 3s |
| 4 | Residents placing sorted foam containers at a collection station | Shows community participation at the individual level | 3-4s |
| 5 | Interior of a Japanese EPS recycling facility, material being processed | Proves the infrastructure exists and operates | 3-4s |
| 6 | Close-up of compacted polystyrene bricks stacked and ready for reuse | Tangible proof of the end product | 2-3s |
| 7 | Logan holding a takeout container, direct to camera, slightly intense expression | Grounds the video in personal advocacy | 3s |
| 8 | Quick montage: US recycling bins with "no styrofoam" stickers or signs | Contrasts the Japan system with US rejection | 3-4s |
| 9 | US landfill with visible foam packaging in the waste stream | Emotional contrast, shows what happens without infrastructure | 2-3s |
| 10 | Map graphic or animation showing Japan's recycling rate vs. US rate | Data visualization for retention and shareability | 3s |
| 11 | Close-up of a US curbside recycling truck driving past foam on the curb | Shows material being left behind by current systems | 2-3s |
| 12 | Japanese recycling education poster or pamphlet in a public area | Demonstrates the education component of the system | 2-3s |
| 13 | Split screen: Japanese foam being recycled vs. US foam in a landfill | Powerful side-by-side that drives the point home | 3-4s |
| 14 | Text overlay: "90%+ recycled" in bold over Japan footage | Retention hook, shareable stat | 2s |
| 15 | Final shot: Logan direct to camera, confident, close framing | Strong personal close for the call to action | 3-4s |
