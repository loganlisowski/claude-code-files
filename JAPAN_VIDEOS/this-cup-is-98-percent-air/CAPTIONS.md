# This Cup Is 98% Air - Caption Variations

---

## Caption 1

This cup is 98% air. Only 2% of it is actual plastic. Expanded polystyrene (EPS) is made by puffing tiny beads with steam until they expand to 50x their original size. That's what makes it so light, so insulating, and so efficient. And when it's time to recycle? You compress the air out and you're left with a dense block of reusable polystyrene. Minimal material. Maximum function.

Save this for the next time someone calls it wasteful.

## Caption 3

Ever hold a styrofoam cup and think about what it's actually made of? It's 98% air. The plastic content is just 2% of the total volume. That tiny amount of polystyrene does everything: insulation, structure, protection. When you recycle EPS, you're compressing out the air and recovering that 2% as a dense, reusable material. More people need to know this.
