## Daily Study Structure (30-45 min)

1. **Review (5 min)** - Quickly revisit prior day's key concepts from memory
2. **Learn (15-20 min)** - Study the day's topic using your prep materials
3. **Practice (10-15 min)** - Work through the practice question prompt and any additional questions
4. **Note (5 min)** - Write down concepts that felt unclear for future review

## Recommended Resources

- Kaplan or Knopman Marks SIE prep book
- FINRA's SIE content outline (official exam blueprint)
- Investopedia for quick concept refreshers
- Practice question banks (Kaplan QBank, ExamFX, or STC)

## SIE Exam Quick Facts

- 75 scored questions + 10 unscored (85 total)
- 105-minute time limit
- Passing score: 70%
- Four content areas:
  - Knowledge of Capital Markets (16%)
  - Understanding Products and Their Risks (44%)
  - Understanding Trading, Customer Accounts, and Prohibited Activities (31%)
  - Overview of the Regulatory Framework (9%)
