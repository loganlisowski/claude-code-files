# This Cup Is 98% Air - Full Script

**Platform:** Instagram Reels (@PolystyreneGuy)
**Target Length:** 45-60 seconds
**Tone:** Educational, casual, mind-blown energy

---

**[HOOK - First 2 seconds]**

[Hold up a styrofoam cup directly to camera]

"This cup is 98% air."

**[Beat 1 - The Breakdown]**

[Rotate the cup slowly]

"Seriously. Expanded polystyrene, the stuff we call styrofoam, is made of tiny polystyrene beads that get puffed up with steam. The result is a material that's 98% air and only 2% actual plastic."

**[Beat 2 - Why That Matters]**

[Set cup down, gesture with hands]

"That's why it's so light. That's why it insulates so well. Your coffee stays hot, your hand stays cool, and the cup weighs almost nothing."

[pause]

"Think about that. Two percent plastic is doing all the work."

**[Beat 3 - The Recycling Angle]**

[Pick cup back up]

"And here's where it gets interesting. Because it's mostly air, when you recycle it, you can compress it down to a fraction of its size. Facilities use heat or mechanical pressure to squeeze the air out, and what's left is a dense block of pure polystyrene that can be reused."

**[Beat 4 - The Takeaway]**

[Direct to camera]

"So next time someone says this stuff is wasteful, remind them: it's 98% air. The material itself is minimal, efficient, and recyclable."

[pause]

"Follow for more facts about the materials you use every day."

**[END]**

---

**Speaker Notes:**
- Hold the cup close to camera on the hook, make it visual
- "98% air" is the sticky fact, repeat it naturally
- On "two percent plastic is doing all the work," slow down slightly for emphasis
- Keep the recycling section moving, don't get too technical
