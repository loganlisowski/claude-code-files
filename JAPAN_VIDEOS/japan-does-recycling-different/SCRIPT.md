# Japan Does Recycling Different - Full Script

**Platform:** Instagram Reels (@PolystyreneGuy)
**Target Length:** 45-60 seconds
**Tone:** Educational, casual, smart friend energy

---

**[HOOK - First 2 seconds]**

"Japan has a separate bin just for polystyrene."

**[Beat 1 - The System]**

[Cut to b-roll of Japanese recycling station]

"In most Japanese cities, residents sort their trash into 10 or more categories. And polystyrene, the stuff your takeout containers and cup noodle bowls are made of, gets its own dedicated category."

[Hold up a styrofoam container]

"That means it doesn't end up in a landfill. It doesn't get mixed with other plastics. It goes straight to a facility designed to process it."

**[Beat 2 - How It Works]**

[Cut to b-roll of collection day in Japan]

"Every neighborhood has a specific collection day for polystyrene. Residents wash it, break it down, and put it out. [pause] And here's the thing, the compliance rate is insane. Over 90% of polystyrene in Japan gets recycled."

**[Beat 3 - The US Comparison]**

[Cut back to camera]

"Meanwhile in the US, most curbside programs don't even accept it. Not because it can't be recycled, but because we never built the infrastructure."

**[Beat 4 - The Takeaway]**

[Direct to camera, slightly closer framing]

"Japan proved it works. The technology exists. The systems exist. We just have to decide we want it."

[pause]

"Follow for more on how we can fix recycling in the US."

**[END]**

---

**Speaker Notes:**
- Keep energy conversational, not preachy
- The hook should feel like you're sharing a surprising fact with a friend
- Emphasize "dedicated category" and "over 90%" with slight vocal punch
- End with conviction, not frustration
