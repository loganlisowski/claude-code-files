# What That Symbol on Your Container Means - B-Roll Shot List

---

| # | Shot Description | Why It Works | Duration |
|---|-----------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Extreme close-up of the #6 resin code on the bottom of a takeout container | The central visual of the entire video, needs to be crisp and readable | 3s |
| 2 | Logan flipping a takeout container upside down to reveal the symbol | Active, engaging motion that viewers can mirror at home | 2-3s |
| 3 | Rapid montage: bottom of a foam cup, egg carton, meat tray, each showing #6 | Shows how common polystyrene is across everyday products | 4-5s |
| 4 | Close-up of a #1 PET water bottle bottom, then a #2 HDPE milk jug | Gives context for the numbering system beyond just #6 | 3s |
| 5 | Finger pointing directly at the number inside the triangle | Directs viewer attention exactly where it needs to go | 2s |
| 6 | Grocery store shelf with foam egg cartons, camera slowly panning | Connects the symbol to a place viewers visit weekly | 3s |
| 7 | Someone at a restaurant casually tossing a foam container in the trash | Shows the problem: people discarding recyclable material unknowingly | 2-3s |
| 8 | Same container being placed into a recycling drop-off bin instead | Shows the solution | 2-3s |
| 9 | A spread of 5-6 different containers laid out, all flipped upside down showing codes | Visual quiz moment, engages the viewer to look closer | 3-4s |
| 10 | Close-up of the recycling triangle vs. the resin code triangle side by side | Drives home that they're different symbols with different meanings | 3s |
| 11 | Logan at a kitchen table sorting containers by number | Makes sorting feel approachable and normal | 3-4s |
| 12 | Text overlay animation zooming into the #6 symbol with "POLYSTYRENE" label | Educational callout that reinforces the key takeaway | 2s |
| 13 | Hands washing a #6 foam container under a faucet | Shows proper prep before recycling | 2-3s |
| 14 | Final shot: Logan holding a container, bottom toward camera, slight smile | Confident closer that matches the hook | 3s |
